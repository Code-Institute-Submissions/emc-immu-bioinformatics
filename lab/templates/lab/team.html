Team

Leticia G. Leon


Leticia is senior postdoc with more than ten years of experience between "wet-lab" and "dry-lab". Now she is fully commit with the bioinformatics part working as main bioinformatician in the Immunology Department at Erasmus MC. She got her PhD degree in Madrid and worked as postdoc in Amsterdam, during her postdoc she decided to follow the Bioinformatic Master program at the VU.

David BH van Zessen
